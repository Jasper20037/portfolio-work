Cute Ghost

During week 8 of the semster i wanted to get ahead with my school work and was super exited to start with 3D (Media Production) that is why i decided to already start practicing. Since it had been some time since the last time i worked in 3D i decided to follow a tutorial to see how much i still remembered. I followed a tutorial from SouthernShotty on youtube. I followed the tutorial and made a cute ghost. I learned a lot from this tutorial and it was a great way to get back into 3D. But ofcourse it was not perfect while working on the ghost i did not weight paint the ghost correctly so the ghost was not moving correctly. But after some online research i found out how to fix the problem i had. When i fixed this and had my first render i asked Jan for feedback. Based on jan's feedback i made some itterations to the animation. But you can learn/see more about the changes i made beneath in the LO3 section.
